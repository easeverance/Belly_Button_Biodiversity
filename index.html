Many bacterial species are not well studied and many are unknown. This case study was intended to document these bacterias. Specifically bacteria that has the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. On the human body there are thousands of types of bacteria. It is believed that the bacteria found in the belly button can produce a taste closest to what real beef tastes like.
